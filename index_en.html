Welcome to ABC !

A warm welcome to you! You have reached the official Website of AnJa's Beach Club!

Location

How to reach us?

🚌 By Bus 🚌

  1. Take the busline 30, 31, 33, 35 or 40 in direction "Travemünde"
  2. Exit the bus at the station "Strandbahnhof"
  3. Cross the street at the trafficlight
  4. Follow the walkway to the beach
  5. On the promenade turn right and folow our signs
  6. 🏖️ After a 5 minute walk you reach the best Beach Club of Germany! 🏖️

🚂 By Train 🚂

  1. Take the train RE8, RE80, RB81 or RB86 from Lübeck City in direction "Travemünde"
  2. Exit the train at the station "Strandbahnhof"
  3. Exit the trainstation to the right
  4. Cross the street at the trafficlight
  5. Follow the walkway to the beach
  6. On the promenade turn right and folow our signs
  7. 🏖️ After a 5 minute walk you reach the best Beach Club of Germany! 🏖️

🚗 By Car 🚗

  1. Take the motorway A1 in direction "Puttgarden"
  2. After Exit 20, change on the motorway A226/B75 in the direction "Travemünde"
  3. Continue to follow the A226
  4. The A226 changes into the B75
  5. Follow the B75 till you reach the citysign "Lübeck-Travemünde"
  6. Turn left into the street "Moorredder"
  7. Head on that street till the end
  8. Turn right into the street "Godewind"
  9. After about 100m you reach our carparkingspace on the right
  10. Cross the street at the trafficlight
  11. Follow the walkway to the beach
  12. On the promenade turn right and folow our signs
  13. 🏖️ After a 5 minute walk you reach the best Beach Club of Germany! 🏖️

You are staying over night?

In Travemünde are several nice places to stay overnight, because every great party has come to an end!

If the weather is perfect and warm you can stay at the great sandbeach and enjoy the waves of the baltic-sea. But be advised: You will find the sand everywhere next morning!

So we recommend to sleep in a real bed. Since we have a good contact to the tourismuscenter of Travemünde, we are proud to give you the following Links! They are presented by Lübeck/Travemünde Tourismus AG!

Events

Wich kinds of events do we have?

Livemusic

We offer several kinds of livemusicartists. From Classicalguitarplayers over great Coversong-Performer to Singer-Songwriter. Every style of music finds a place in our Beach Club.

We also do a Open-Mic-Competion every 3rd Friday a month, and the Winner don't have to pay the bill for this evening!

Feel free to find out wich musician is playing next, by checking our facebook-Page !

Themepartys

We also hold theme nights at irregular intervals. From the well-known "Ballermann Party", over the "Sexy Seductive Single Day" to the "Well-Dressed Party Day", we have everything.

During the summer months there is also always a karaoke party where you can dress up like real superstars to get a 10% discount on your bill!

Chillout

During the weekdays, we will provide you with relaxing and beautiful "chill-out lounge music" while you enjoy your drinks.

We always pay attention to your mood and let our in-house DJ's play other music if necessary!
